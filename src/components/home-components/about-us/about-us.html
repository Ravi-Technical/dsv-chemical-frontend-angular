About Our Organization

D.S.V. Chemicals is a leading manufacturer and exporters of synthetic resins and has established itself as a dependable supplier of synthetic resins in the Indian and international market.

D.S.V. Chemicals offers a wide selection of products for the surface coating industry covering decorative and industrial paints, protective coatings, printing inks and adhesives.

D.S.V. works in a close partnership with its customers offering tailor-made solutions to meet their specific requirements.
